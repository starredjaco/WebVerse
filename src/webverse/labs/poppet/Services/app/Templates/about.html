Toys Made to Last a Childhood

Poppet was founded in 2019 in a converted tobacco barn on the outskirts of Asheville. What started as a weekend market booth selling hand-carved animals has grown into a small studio of designers, woodworkers, and textile artists — all committed to making toys that don't need batteries.

We believe the best toys are simple, beautiful, and built to survive years of love. Every piece we ship is made from responsibly sourced materials by people whose names we know.

What We Believe

Natural Materials First

Appalachian hardwoods, organic cotton, beeswax finishes. We never use plastic, and every material is non-toxic and child-safe.

Small Batches Only

We produce in runs of 12 to 50 pieces. This keeps quality high and lets us iterate designs based on real feedback.

Designed to be Repaired

Loose joints, faded paint, torn seams — we offer free lifetime repairs on everything we make. Toys should outlive trends.
